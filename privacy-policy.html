Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

Your financial data belongs to you — and it stays with you. Here is a plain-language explanation of how this site handles information.
1

100% Browser-Based Processing (Data Isolation)

Calcs Made Simple operates entirely inside your local web browser. Every number, asset value, and cost calculation you type stays inside your device's temporary memory. We do not transmit, collect, see, or store any of your financial inputs on any remote server. Your sensitive financial models never leave your computer.

2

Essential Local Storage and Session Cookies

This website uses basic browser storage tokens solely to keep your calculation values intact when you switch back and forth between different calculator screens. These tokens do not track your internet history, cannot read files on your computer, and disappear automatically when you close your browser tab.

3

Anonymous Site Performance Statistics

We may use lightweight, anonymous scripts to measure page loading speeds and look for layout bugs. These tools only measure general website health and cannot collect your name, location, email address, or any personal details.

4

Encrypted Contact Communications

If you choose to use our Contact Us form, your email address and message are instantly encrypted over a secure connection (HTTPS) before being routed to our private inbox. This data is used exclusively to answer your question and is never shared, rented, or sold to third-party marketing companies.
